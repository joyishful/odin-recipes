Perfect Pancakes

Description

Serve these pancakes with butter and syrup.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat a griddle.
  2. Stir together milk and vinegar in a small bowl.
  3. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a second bowl.
  4. Flip pancakes and cook until browned on the other side.
